FAQs About the Assembly Audit

How is this different from a standard design review?

A standard review checks if a part meets drawing dimensions. Our audit simulates how that part behaves in your assembly, identifying dynamic failures (like jamming or slipping) that static checks cannot predict.

Is this an on-site or remote service?

Both. We typically begin with a remote audit based on your drawings and data. If the issue is complex, an on-site review of the physical assembly line can be arranged to gather real-world data.

What is the final deliverable?

You will receive a detailed PDF report featuring visual overlays of risk zones, a root cause analysis for each potential failure mode, and actionable design or process recommendations to fix them.

What data do you need from us to start?

To start, we need the 2D/3D CAD files of the problematic joint, information on materials and coatings, and a brief description of the failure you are observing. Video of the failure is often very helpful.
