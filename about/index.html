We Exist to Eliminate Assembly Failure.

We are not a typical fastener manufacturer. We are an engineering team obsessed with the power of geometry. We believe 99% of assembly failures—jamming, vibration loosening, and unexpected breaks—originate from geometric flaws smaller than 0.1mm that are overlooked in the design stage. Our mission is to find and fix them before they become your costly reality.

A Message from Our Lead Engineer

"I've spent 10+ years in non-standard parts manufacturing. I've seen too many 'theoretically perfect' designs become disasters on the assembly line. I started Gorgeo Fasteners with one belief: a part's value is not whether it matches a drawing's dimensions, but whether it perfectly performs its function in the final assembly. We are obsessed with the ±0.01mm that makes the difference, because we know that's where reliability is born."

— Catherine Zhang, Principal Engineer & Lead Consultant
